# service/ — work as service domain

Single-agent domain. The service agent helps Avry think about WORK — consulting, Journey AI Group, agency engagements, client management, offers, pricing, deliverables, sales conversations.

## Folder shape

- `SKILL.md` — agent definition
- `references/` — distilled thinking on offer design, pricing models, client management, sales frameworks, case studies, lessons from past engagements
- `assets/` — proposal templates, SOW templates, intake forms, pricing calculators
- `scripts/` — (later) project cost calculator, invoice generator

## Why "service" as a top-level domain

Avry's work IS service — value delivered to clients in exchange for money. Treating it as a life pillar (alongside God, Body, Family, Homebase, Finances) reinforces that work is meaningful contribution, not just income generation.

This is distinct from:
- **Finances** (the money flow — accounts, runway, taxes). Finances tracks the OUTPUT of service work.
- **Family** (private life). Service is the public/professional sphere.
- **Each specific venture** (Journey AI Group has its own brain at `~/Developer/venture-journey-ai-group/brain/`). The service agent thinks about the SHAPE of work across ventures — what offers to make, how to price, when to take on a client, when to refer.

## What lives here vs in venture brains

- **HERE:** principles, frameworks, lessons that transfer across all consulting engagements. Pricing philosophy, ICP thinking, offer design, sales conversation patterns, when to say no.
- **IN VENTURE BRAINS:** specific clients, specific engagements, specific products. Each venture (Journey AI Group, future ventures) owns its own brain.

The service agent reads ITS OWN references but can be asked questions that span ventures.
