## 2025-07

### Jul 28 - Girls Who Don't Open Up

Girls who don't let themselves open up. They don't go deep with people. Keep to the surface. Give one word answers or short surface level answers. Don't really share stories about their life.

This can be because they've been hurt before. They say "don't trust people." One of the reasons is past relationships. If a guy hurts them it may cause them to not get into another relationship for 4+ years. To not allow themselves to fall for somebody else.

**Insight:** You have to recognize this pattern early. It's not about your approach being wrong - it's about her walls being up from damage. Different from a girl who's just not interested (she'll disengage entirely). The guarded girl still engages but at surface level.

**New questions that worked:**
- "What's the best hotel experience you've ever had?" vs "If you could go to one hotel and do anything you wanted in it, what would you do?" - Second version forces imagination, way more fun and creative. Facts vs imagination.
- "What's your biggest turn off? Ok, and what are your biggest turn ons?"
- "You know I've noticed something about you.." (pause, let curiosity build)

### Aug 13 - Texting Principles

Four principles from real texting conversations:

1. **Evoke massive curiosity from your hook.** Make the girl so curious from the start that she has to respond.

2. **Flip the script.** Frame it as her chasing you. You are the innocent one. You want to take things slow and just meet the girl. She's the one taking things too fast, she's the one that's just wanting to hook up with you. She's the one trying to kidnap you.

3. **Always advance.** You're not here to be her texting buddy. Girls text other girls to communicate. Girls text guys to get to hangout. They text guys who are direct in their communication, not guys who are just texting to text. Always be advancing the conversation forward or sidestepping.

4. **Read the subtext.** See when she asks logistical questions (she's interested in meeting). See when she's shit testing you (she's testing if you're real). Understand how to pass shit tests: agree and amplify, ignore and redirect, playfully deflect, pressure flip / throw it back, stay unfazed (stoic vibes). Don't be afraid to give girls shit tests too.

**Bonus:** You can respond to a question with a question.

### Aug 15 - Handling "I Don't Want To Have Sex"

Understanding comes before explanation. Only when you understand something fully can you truly explain it.

Context: Tinder girl came over, started hooking up, she said she didn't want to have sex. Here's what worked:

**Step 1: Lower the guard.** "That's totally ok, we're not going to do anything that you don't want to do." Be sincere - because it's genuinely the truth. This drops the walls.

**Step 2: Understand why.** While the guard is lowered, ask curiously why she doesn't want to. She opened up: worried about people in her hometown finding out she was "just sleeping around." Didn't want to feel like a hoe.

**Step 3: Handle the objection.** Helped her realize nobody would find out. Separated the sex from the judgment. "If they were never going to find out, would you want to?" - separating what she actually wants from the external pressure she's putting on herself.

**Step 4: She decides.** You can only get a person to do something they want to do. Women want sex. If she says no, there's an objection underneath. Lower the guard, find it, handle it, and let her make the choice.

**Principle:** This is sales applied to intimacy. Lower the guard, find the real objection, handle it, let them decide. Same framework Max uses in business.

### Aug 16 - Escalation Framework

**Core insight: She's a dial, not a switch.** You turn up the dial and then give a girl what you both know you want to do.

Two parallel tracks of escalation:

1. **Physical escalation** - touching her thighs, caressing her arm, kissing her neck, kissing her body, making out. Each step leads and hints at sex happening.

2. **Verbal escalation** - get her in the mood with words. Sexual innuendo, hinting at sex without directly saying it.

**Examples of verbal escalation (from Max's style):**
- She says she sleeps with ASMR and asks if you want to see how she sleeps: "I would love to find out how you sleep"
- She talks about sending a picture in her work uniform: "You can send me that picture of you in your uniform, or you without it too"

**Insight:** Max is really good at bringing up sex within conversations naturally. The key is making the sexual subtext feel effortless, not forced. Verbal and physical escalation should run in parallel - each amplifies the other.

### Sep 7 - Reading Girls Through Their Gossip

You can start to get a sense of the girls you're talking to by:
- Who they talk about when they're not here
- What their gossip is about
- What issues they share that they're having
- What they complain about
- What they show they are disgusted by
- What they judge about others and about life events

**Real example:** Luz was sharing about how Florencia (her content creator friend) didn't communicate properly when she left Luz on read. They were figuring out plans to hang out and Florencia didn't just flat out say "no" she didn't want to go out. That came off as being a bitch, made Luz upset, and made Luz want her to communicate better and just say no.

**What this tells you:** Luz values direct communication and honesty. She gets frustrated by passive behavior. She holds her friends to standards. These are character signals.
