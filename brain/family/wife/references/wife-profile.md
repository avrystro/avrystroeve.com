### The Shit Test
The shit test is a test to see if you're actually the shit.
- The minute the girl sees that you're saying and doing things to make her think a certain way, you're done
- If the girl has any sense that your self worth is attached to her perception of you - instantly done
- What the shit test is: she's gonna give a perception of you and see if you fall into that perception and that frame
- "I'm doing this for you"
- My goal is to make the girl feel so good and safe

You can fractionate the vibe you're coming from. The 4 vibes are:

**1. Dominant / Strong / Leader**
You set the direction and you're in the lead. Not domineering like arrogance and no care for others.
- Belief: "I'm not your girlfriend with a penis. At some point I could fuck you and I'm also going to make sure you're safe every step of the way."

**3. Demonstrating Authority / Understanding**
The parallel part is having genuine deep curiosity about her world.

**4. Vulnerable / Authentic**
You speak your truth - if she pisses you off you tell her.
