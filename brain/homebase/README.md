## Why "homebase" not "place"

"Place" is about location (where Avry lives). Homebase is about MAKING a place livable AND sovereign — the active practice of building, growing, watering, generating, hosting. Place-research / travel / vacation belongs elsewhere (likely a future `places/` domain or under `god/` for spiritual geography).
